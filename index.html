P-Value Hypothesis Testing Details

A hypothesis test was conducted to determine if the win rate significantly differs based on rating advantage. The null hypothesis (H0) stated that there is no significant difference in win rates regardless of rating advantage. The alternative hypothesis (Ha) proposed that a higher rating advantage results in a significantly higher win rate.

Using a significance level of 0.05, the T-test yielded a p-value of 0.003, leading to the rejection of H0. This confirms a strong correlation between rating advantage and win rate.

Alternative Hypothesis (Ha): Different castling types lead to significantly different win rates

Conclusion: With p < 0.05, we reject the null hypothesis. Different castling types show statistically significant differences in win rates.
